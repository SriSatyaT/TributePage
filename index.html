Satya Narayana Nadella

CEO of Microsoft

Early Life of Satya Nadella

  • 1988 Nadella attended the Hyderabad Public School, Begumpet before receiving a bachelor's in electrical engineering from the Manipal Institute of Technology in Karnataka.

  • 1990 Nadella then traveled to the U.S. to study for an M.S. in computer science at the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee, receiving his degree.

  • 1997 Nadella received an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business .

  • 1992 Nadella worked at Sun Microsystems as a member of its technology staff before joining Microsoft

  • 4 February 2014 Nadella was announced as the new CEO of Microsoft.

“Many companies aspire to change the world. But very few have all the elements required: talent, resources and perseverance. Microsoft has proven that it has all three in abundance.”

Today's Microsoft

Most recently, Nadella was executive vice president of Microsoft’s Cloud and Enterprise group. In this role he led the transformation to the cloud infrastructure and services business, which outperformed the market and took share from competition. Previously, Nadella led R&D for the Online Services Division and was vice president of the Microsoft Business Division. Before joining Microsoft, Nadella was a member of the technology staff at Sun Microsystems.

Joining Microsoft in 1992, he quickly became known as a leader who could span a breadth of technologies and businesses to transform some of Microsoft’s biggest product offerings.
